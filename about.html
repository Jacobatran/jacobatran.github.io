Statement

I compose work that concern in augment of human experiences. I am attentive to enhance mankind competencies and the gift of contentment. I desire creation that lead to an expansion of sensibility and provides delights for individualism through merging the gap of digital and reality through unifying art and technology.

The website design, creative thinking and visual arts I created are with the intention to alleviate the pain of frustration and/or to attained amusement.

These projects range from a simple easy to navigate resourceful website which delivered an honest in-depth tech review to help conscious consumer purchase to the creative thinking behind a user-interface app that would solve the problem for a current affair of food delivery, a high demand service for public needs.

While I am continuing on my journey, I hope to cultivate my discipline to advance humanity from the burden of chaos to a more delightful experience. The work I make will be aligned with the range of organizations that impose those same core values and practices.
